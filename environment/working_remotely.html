Working remotely¶

  1. The Poldracklab has a long history of remote members, and we support the needs of lab members who must work remotely.

  2. Any remote work must be discussed with and approved by Dr. Poldrack and arranged in accordance with Stanford University Policies on Staff Telecommuting and Remote Working.
