Research Projects

Cognitive neuroscience projects

Characterizing cognitive control networks using a precision neuroscience approach

  • A project that is characterizing the large-scale brain networks involved in diverse cognitive control processes using deep phenotyping.
  • Funded by 5R01MH117772
  • Papers:
    • [A dual-task approach to inform the taxomony of inhibition-related processes] (https://psycnet.apa.org/record/2023-31463-001)

Data-driven validation of cognitive RDoC dimensions using deep phenotyping

  • A project that is reframing the Cognitive Systems component of RDoC using deep phenotyping with behavior and fMRI.
  • Funded by 1R01MH130898

Precise brain maps of motor skill training in humans

  • A project investigating how functional connectivity changes within individuals over the course of learning a new motor skill
  • Funded by the Wu Tsai Human Performance Alliance]

Neural network models of human performance

  • A project that is developing end-to-end neural network models for the dynamics of human performance on cognitive control tasks
  • Publications:
    • Modelling human behaviour in cognitive tasks with latent dynamical systems

Challenging existing models and developing new models for response inhibition

  • A project aimed at development next-generation models of response inhibition
  • Publications: -Severe violations of independence in response inhibition tasks -[Estimating the time to do nothing: Towards next-generation models of response inhibition (https://journals.sagepub.com/doi/10.1177/09637214221121753

Applying novel technologies and methods to inform the ontology of self-regulation

  • A study examining the organization of cognitive functions that underlie self-regulatory abilities
  • Funded by 5UH3DA041713
  • Publications:
    • Uncovering the structure of self-regulation through data-driven ontology discovery
    • Large-scale analysis of test–retest reliabilities of self-regulation measures
    • Applying Novel Technologies and Methods to Inform the Ontology of Self-Regulation
    • Relating psychiatric symptoms and self-regulation during the COVID-19 crisis
    • Momentary Influences on Self-Regulation in Two Populations With Health Risk Behaviors: Adults Who Smoke and Adults Who Are Overweight and Have Binge-Eating Disorder
    • A Psychometric Analysis of the Brief Self-Control Scale
    • The cognitive and perceptual correlates of ideological attitudes: a data-driven approach
    • Correlation Database of 60 Cross-Disciplinary Surveys and Cognitive Tasks Assessing Self-Regulation
    • Measurement in Intensive Longitudinal Data

MyConnectome

  • A project that densely characterized brain function in a single individual over the course of 18 months.
  • Publications:
    • See myconnectome.org for a full list of publications arising from this project

Informatics projects

OpenNeuro

  • A data sharing archive for neuroscience data
  • Funded by R24MH117179
  • Publications:
    • The OpenNeuro resource for sharing of neuroscience data

The impact of AI on Neuroimaging Data Privacy

  • A project investigating the effectiveness of deidentification in the context of AI face recognition systems
  • Funded by the Stanford Human-Centered Artificial Intelligence Institute

Brain Imaging Data Structure (BIDS)

  • A framework for organization of neuroimaging data and metadata
  • Funded by R01MH126699, R24MH114705
  • Publications:
    • The brain imaging data structure, a format for organizing and describing outputs of neuroimaging experiments

Experiment Factory

  • A platform for online behavioral experimentation
  • Funded by Columbia University Science of Behavior Change Resource and Coordinating Center
  • Publications:
    • The Experiment Factory: Standardizing Behavioral Experiments

Neurosynth

  • A platform for coordinate-based meta-analysis on neuroimaging data
  • Funded by 5R01MH096906
  • Publications:
    • Large-scale automated synthesis of human functional neuroimaging data

fMRIPrep

  • A robust and reproducible preprocessing workflow for fMRI data
  • Funded by RF1MH121867
  • Publications:
    • fMRIPrep: a robust preprocessing pipeline for functional MRI

Women’s Brain Health Initiative Data Coordinating Center

  • A project to coordinate data from several University of California Imaging Centers in service of better understanding women’s brain health.

Cognitive Atlas

  • An ontology for cognitive science
  • Funded by 5R01MH082795
  • Publications:
    • The cognitive atlas: toward a knowledge foundation for cognitive neuroscience

Neurovault

  • A data sharing archive for summary data from neuroimaging studies
  • Publications:
    • NeuroVault.org: a web-based repository for collecting and sharing unthresholded statistical maps of the human brain

MRIQC

  • A quality control tool for structural and functional MRI data
  • Publications:
    • MRIQC: Advancing the automatic prediction of image quality in MRI from unseen sites

Templateflow

  • A FAIR sharing archive and toolkit for neuroimaging templates and atlases
  • Publications:
    • TemplateFlow: FAIR-sharing of multi-scale, multi-species brain models
